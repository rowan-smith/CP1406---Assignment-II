Our Orchestra's Supporters

Why We Need You

There are many expenses involved in upkeeping an orchestra, even one that is populated by volunteers such as our own. The cost of venue booking alone takes a large sum of money, but there's numerous smaller expenses that need to be handled as well. Equipment maintenence, registration fees, and the occasional flight for one of our talented guest musicians. These all add up, and it's only thanks to our generous supporters that we can continue to devote so much personal time to giving them the best musical performance we can. In recognition of everyones contributions, here is a list of thanks to everyone who has supported us over the years. We wouldn't be here today if not for you, and thank you for believing in us. Also, if anyone wished to help us further please head over to our support form and submit your details.

Our Sponsors

Sponsors are businesses that are providing regular donations to help keep our orchestra going. If you have a business that would like to support us by becoming a sponsor, please contact us using the information listed at the bottom of the page.

Townsville City Council


The major sponsor behind the Barrier Reef Orchestra. The Townsville City Council provides funds as a way to encourage cultural tourism. As having an extra local attraction helps bring more visitors to our city and helps our music reach more ears, the patronage of the Council is a much valued accordance.
